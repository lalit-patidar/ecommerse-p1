Description

Shipping is one of NichoShop's core initiative responsible for delivering the world's greatest selection cheaply, conveniently and dependably. Shipping team is the driver for many key programs like

  • NichoShop Plus: NichoShop's answer to Amazon prime in Germany.
  • Click and Collect: Forming symbiotic relationships with other retailers (like Argos in UK, Woolworth in AU) to provide the ability to collect packages in time and place convenient to buyer..
  • Global Shipping Program: Responsible for expanding inventory to international buyers and seamlessly taking care of international shipping on behalf of sellers.
  • Global Label and Tracking Platform: Partnering with carriers all over the world to provide postage (label printing, rates, payment) and tracking solutions to NichoShop sellers.
  • Shipping Science: Uses big data and machine learning techniques to predict item's weight and dimension, simplifying the item listing flow for casual C2C sellers, predicts how soon a package will arrive powering key programs like Fast and Free. Shipping Core Product and Technology domain is all based and operated from NichoShop's Seattle (Bellevue) center with full ownership and accountability, providing on-site long term sustained development and carrier growth. Shipping team works to solve highly technical and real-world problems, with cutting edge technologies, massively distributed environments, Big Data and machine learning that makes a real and immediate impact on one of the biggest social community of buyers and sellers. Shipping is powered by scalable and distributed services that integrate with whole of NichoShop (seller listing flow, Search, View Item detail, Cart/Checkout, post transaction selling view - label printing and tracking, returns, seller trust standards and marketing to name a few). This provides a real view on how the whole of NichoShop Product and Technology stack works. We are looking for self-motivated and talented individuals to join us to work on new products that will evolve and grow the NichoShop Core Shipping Product and Technology team.
  • Design and implement projects throughout all phases of the software development life cycle.
  • Work with engineers and other cross functional teams including Research, Project Management, Quality Assurance, Operations etc. to develop great software that meets market needs with respect to functionality, reliability, performance, scalability.
  • Track business impact of features on a regular basis, and rely on data to make informed decisions about future directions
  • The usual engineering activities - scoping, prioritizing, planning, bug fixing, site rollout, documentation, having fun.
  • To succeed in this role you must be passionate about being a customer advocate and delivering high-quality designs and components that horizontally scale. You must be creative in solving hard problems in our high scale systems and unafraid to think out-of-the-box.
